OUR MISSION

Understanding the World
Through Sound

Auralis is an advanced Audio Language Model (ALM) designed to process, understand, and reason about speech and environmental audio simultaneously. While visual AI sees the world, Auralis hears it.

Dual Processing

Unlike traditional models, we process speech semantics and acoustic features in parallel for 30% faster inference.

Context Aware

We don't just transcribe text; we identify location, emotion, and environmental context (traffic, nature, indoor).

Privacy First

Audio is processed on-edge where possible. We do not store voice data longer than the session requires.
